About Us

E Cube solutions consists of a staunch team of serial renewable energy enthusiasts who have a passion for solar energy products & services. Team E Cube is steadfast in its bold mission: to foster sustainable development and to create a better and cleaner earth for future generations by bringing electricity powered by the sun to millions of people worldwide. We have grown into one of Pakistan’s swiftly expanding renewable energy company’s and have evolved into the preferred choice for the procurement of solar photovoltaic products and energy solutions for domestic | commercial | industrial sector.
